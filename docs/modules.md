# Platform Modules

## Core Functional Modules

The marketplace platform was structured around the following core modules:

- Supplier Management
- Product Catalog Management
- Category Management
- Inventory and Warehouse Operations
- Customer Order Processing
- Delivery and Tracking
- KPI and Incentive Reporting
- Audit and Monitoring

## Supplier Management
Supports supplier onboarding, supplier reference data, and supplier-product relationships.

## Product Catalog Management
Supports SKU registration, product categorization, multilingual product structure, and pricing reference data.

## Category Management
Supports hierarchical product grouping for marketplace navigation and category expansion.

## Inventory and Warehouse Operations
Supports stock balance visibility, inventory reservation, warehouse allocation, and stock movement history.

## Customer Order Processing
Supports customer order creation, order items, order amount calculation, and order lifecycle statuses.

## Delivery and Tracking
Supports delivery order creation, courier assignment, QR tracking, GPS tracking, and delivery status management.

## KPI and Incentive Reporting
Supports operational performance measurement for warehouses, couriers, and other operational roles.

## Audit and Monitoring
Supports traceability of key system actions, API request logging, and business event monitoring.
