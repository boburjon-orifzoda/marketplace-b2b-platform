# KPI Engine

## Purpose
The KPI engine was designed to track operational and employee performance across different departments and to support incentive-based compensation models.

## Tracked Roles
- Sales Managers
- Operations Staff
- Couriers
- Supervisors

## KPI Categories
- order volume
- product sales
- delivery count
- fulfillment speed
- conversion performance
- daily productivity
- new client acquisition
- incentive thresholds

## Sales Manager Logic
Sales managers can receive percentage-based incentives based on:
- product sales performance
- total sales volume
- client acquisition
- continued customer activity

## Courier Logic
Couriers can receive performance-based incentives depending on operational thresholds such as completed delivery count.

## Rule Configuration
Performance thresholds and percentage rules are configured according to business stakeholder requirements.

## Reporting Use Cases
- employee performance dashboards
- courier productivity monitoring
- sales team target tracking
- client acquisition tracking
- operational efficiency reporting
