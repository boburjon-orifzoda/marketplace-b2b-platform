# KPI Engine

## Purpose
The KPI engine was designed to track operational and employee performance across different departments.

## Tracked Roles
- Sales Managers
- Operations Staff
- Couriers
- Supervisors

## KPI Categories
- order volume
- delivery count
- fulfillment speed
- conversion performance
- daily productivity
- incentive thresholds

## Incentive Logic
Performance incentives are calculated based on predefined thresholds and percentage rules configured by business stakeholders.

## Reporting Use Cases
- employee performance dashboards
- courier productivity monitoring
- sales team target tracking
- operational efficiency reporting
