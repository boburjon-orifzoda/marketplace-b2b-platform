# Integration Flow

## External Integrations
- ERP / inventory systems
- Delivery provider APIs
- AI-powered call center
- GPS tracking services
- Reporting / BI tools

## Integration Scenarios

### ERP Sync
Product, inventory, and operational data are synchronized with internal enterprise systems.

### Delivery Provider Integration
Courier dispatch requests are sent to logistics partners for order pickup and delivery execution.

### AI Call Center
Call center workflows support operational communication and customer service scenarios.

### Tracking Integration
Delivery progress is monitored through tracking services and reflected in platform workflows.

### Reporting Flow
Operational data is transferred into analytics / reporting layers for KPI tracking and management dashboards.
